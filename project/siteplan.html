MyQuiz Site Plan

Site Name

MyQuiz - This name represents an interactive quiz platform where users can test their knowledge on various topics.

Site Purpose

The site provides an engaging and educational platform where users can select quizzes based on their interests, receive instant feedback on their answers, and enhance their learning experience through interactive features.

Scenarios

  • How can I test my knowledge on different topics?
  • Can I get instant feedback and correct answers after answering a question?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #000 (Used for headings and buttons)

Secondary Color: #F2F2F2 (Used for backgrounds and section dividers)

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto (Used for body text and headings)

Secondary Font: Roboto (Used for call-to-action elements and special sections)
